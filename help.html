LMIS Summary
 
1. Introduction to LMIS
LMIS stands for Labour Market Information System, Where it generally contribute to the following pillars
1.1. Recruiting graduates / job seekers by employers (connected with training/career guidance/education)
1.2. 2- Training services connected to employers / job seekers (also intelligent to give advice on training)
1.3. 4- Career guidance and counselling (online and face to face) connected to training and job seeking / employers
These three pillars are interconnected on the LMIS, the LMIS unlike the normal job-market and simple matchmaking keeps tracking of the job-seeker life cycle from education / graduation through working experience the LMIS is concerned with the issue of
 Unemployment /
 Job creation /
 policy decision support for the labour market ecosystem /
 which means the continuous analysis of LMI /
 Current and future Jobs/
 Current and future Seekers/
 Current and future employer /
 Current and future investment & industry needs)
 Tracer studies from education -> graduation -> job seeking -> employed ->
 Signal analysis from the labour market
