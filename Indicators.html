Egypt Employed Persons

The number of employed persons in Egypt increased to 28070 Thousand in the second quarter of 2019 from 25701 Thousand in the first quarter of 2019. Employed Persons in Egypt averaged 22823.89 Thousand from 2003 until 2019, reaching an all time high of 28070 Thousand in the second quarter of 2019 and a record low of 18284 Thousand in the first
quarter of 2003.

Unemployment Rate

معدل البطالة


source: tradingeconomics.com

...

The unemployment rate in Egypt fell to 7.5 percent in the second quarter of 2019 compared to 9.9 percent in the same period a year earlier and 8.1 percent in the previous quarter. This is the lowest jobless rate since comparable series began in 2003, as the number of unemployed persons declined sharply by 781 thousand to 2.09 million while employed rose by 1,909 thousand to 28.1 million. Unemployment Rate in Egypt averaged 10.79 percent from 1993 until 2019, reaching an all time high of 13.40 percent in the third quarter of 2013 and a record low of 7.50 percent in the second quarter of 2019.
